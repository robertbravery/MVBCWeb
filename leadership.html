Our Leadership

Elders

Brady Farr, Francois Jardine, and Douglas Steytler are the elder-pastors of MountainView Bbile Church. Brady is an American church-planting missionary with Biblical Ministries Worldwide, who has been serving in pastoral ministry in the U.S and South Africa for the last twenty years. Francois grew up in South Africa, played rugby with the Blue Bulls and the Lions, and has served in pastoral ministry in several other churches before coming to serve at MountainView. Douglas is Executive Director of Christian Mission International, the Southern African branch of The Voice of the Martyrs. He has been faithfully serving in church leadership for many years.

Deacons

MountainView also has four deacons who serve for one year terms. These men carry much of the weight of the ministry at MountainView. They are responsible for member care, building care, church finances and coordinating church programs and events.
