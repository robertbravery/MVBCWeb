OUR CORE VALUES

We Are Committed to God's Word

We believe the Bible is the Word of God and that its principles are sufficient to guide us in everything we do, as individuals and as a fellowship.

We Are Committed to God

Together, we want to glorify God through worship, prayer, and celebration of the Lord's Supper and baptism. Individually, we want to live out our devotion to God through prayer, scripture memory, and Bible study, striving to deny ourselves and display a life of integrity, sacrifice, and character in our pursuit of Christlikeness.

We Are Committed to Fellow Believers

We highly value unity, fellowship, and meeting each other's spiritual and physical needs.

We Are Committed to Evangelism

We place a high priority on reaching our community with the gospel in a personal and relevant way.

We Are Committed to Discipleship

We have a passion to enhance the spiritual growth of every believer through life-on-life relationships in order to bring them to maturity in Christ and equip them to lead and serve others.

We Are Committed To Learning, Flexibility And Innovation

While preserving the biblical truths and traditions of the New Testament church, we desire to be life-long learners and innovators in reaching people with the gospel in an ethnically and culturally diverse society.

Quick Information:

A Bible church who love and respect God's Word. Our goal is to help people begin a relationship with Jesus Christ. We have a passion to enhance the spiritual growth of every believer through life-on-life relationships.
